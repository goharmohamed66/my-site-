Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tech Operation dashboard ("we", "our", "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our internal advertising and store-management tool.

1. Who we are

The App is a private internal dashboard used by Tech Operation team members and authorized clients to manage their own WooCommerce stores, shipping accounts, and Meta (Facebook) advertising accounts. It is not a public consumer product.

2. Information we access

When you connect a Meta (Facebook) account to the App, we request the following permissions strictly to perform the actions you initiate:

  • Public profile (your name and ID) — to display who is connected.
  • ads_read — to read campaigns, ad sets, ads, and insights from ad accounts you administer.
  • ads_management — to create, pause, edit, or duplicate campaigns/ads when you explicitly request it from the dashboard.
  • business_management — to list and access the business and ad accounts you already own.

The App does not access any data outside the ad accounts and businesses that you yourself administer.

3. How we store information

The long-lived access token returned by Facebook is stored encrypted at rest on our private server (Hostinger), accessible only to authorized team members. Account names, IDs, and ad-performance metrics are cached in our database to render reports faster.

4. How we use the information

The information is used solely to:

  • Display the ad accounts you have connected.
  • Pull spend, reach, and ROAS metrics into the in-app reports you request.
  • Execute the campaign / ad operations you explicitly trigger from the dashboard (e.g. duplicate a campaign, change a budget, pause an ad).

We never sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party.

5. Data retention

Tokens and cached account metadata remain stored as long as the account is connected. You can revoke access at any time:

  • From within the App — open Settings → Ads → click Delete on the connection.
  • From Facebook — Settings → Apps and Websites → remove the App.

Once revoked, all related tokens and cached data are deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

6. Cookies

The App uses only essential session cookies required for login and security. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies on this dashboard.

7. Children's privacy

The App is an internal business tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or to request data deletion, email: techopration72@gmail.com.
